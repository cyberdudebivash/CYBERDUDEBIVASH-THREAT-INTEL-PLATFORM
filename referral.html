FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to be a paying customer to refer?
No. Anyone can generate a referral link and start referring. However, your referrals are more likely to convert if you're an active user who can speak to the platform's value firsthand. Free Community tier users are welcome to participate.
Does the referred person get a discount too?
Yes — referred users get an additional 10% off their first month when they sign up through your referral link. This is on top of any existing free trial offers. Your referral link automatically applies the discount at checkout.
What happens after the 12-month commission period?
Standard affiliate commissions apply for the first 12 months of each referred subscriber's paid plan. After 12 months, the commission ends — the customer is now considered a long-term subscriber. If you want to earn ongoing residual income without a time limit, consider our MSSP White-Label Program where commission applies for the life of your managed client relationships.
Can I refer my organization's own subscription?
Self-referrals are not permitted. The referred account must be a genuinely new customer who has not previously had a paid subscription. Violations result in permanent commission forfeiture and may result in account termination.
How do I track my referral conversions?
Email affiliate@cyberdudebivash.com with your referral link after generating it to receive access to your affiliate dashboard. The dashboard shows real-time clicks, signups, trial starts, and confirmed paid conversions with commission amounts. We also send a monthly earnings summary by email.
What if someone uses my link but pays later?
Referral cookies last 90 days. If a user clicks your link, starts a free trial, and converts to a paid subscription at any point within 90 days, the conversion is credited to you. After 90 days, the attribution window closes and any subsequent purchase is not attributed.
Is there a limit on how many people I can refer?
No limit. Refer 1 person or 100 — the commission rate is the same (20% for 12 months) for every valid conversion. Security bloggers, community managers, and conference speakers often refer dozens of subscribers per month.
What if I want a larger commission for high-volume referrals?
If you can refer 5+ Enterprise subscribers or 20+ PRO subscribers per month, contact us at enterprise@cyberdudebivash.com to discuss a custom affiliate arrangement or to join our formal Partner Program for higher revenue share tiers.
