Frequently Asked Questions

What is a "god mode dossier"?
A 20-section enterprise tactical intelligence report: executive summary, CVSS 3.1 decomposition, EPSS analysis, MITRE ATT&CK mapping, IOC enrichment, Sigma/KQL/SPL detection rules, FAIR financial model, campaign intelligence, strategic risk, AI narrative, regulatory compliance, and remediation roadmap. Typical size: 70-90KB.
How do I import STIX 2.1 into my SIEM?
STIX 2.1 bundles work with Splunk ES (TAXII connector), Microsoft Sentinel (Threat Intelligence Platforms), Elastic SIEM (TAXII2 module), and any TAXII 2.1-compatible platform. We provide import guides for all major SIEMs.
What CVEs do you cover?
All CRITICAL (9.0+) CVEs in widely-deployed software receive god mode treatment within 24-48 hours of disclosure. HIGH (7.0-8.9) CVEs affecting enterprise infrastructure are covered within 72 hours.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — 7 days free on Analyst and Team plans, no credit card required. Enterprise plans offer a proof-of-concept pack: 3 god mode reports across severity levels, with a sample SLA document.
