Frequently Asked Questions

What is a “God Mode” dossier?
A 20-section enterprise tactical intelligence report per CVE: executive summary, CVSS 3.1 vector decomposition, EPSS exploitation probability, MITRE ATT&CK mapping, IOC enrichment tables, Sigma/KQL/SPL detection rules, FAIR financial impact model, ransomware campaign intelligence, strategic risk narrative, AI executive summary, regulatory compliance guidance, and a full remediation roadmap. Typical report: 70–90 KB. Delivered within 24–48h of CRITICAL disclosure.
How do I import STIX 2.1 bundles into my SIEM?
STIX 2.1 bundles are consumable via TAXII 2.1 by Splunk ES (TAXII connector), Microsoft Sentinel (Threat Intelligence Platforms data connector), Elastic SIEM (TAXII2 module), IBM QRadar (STIX adapter), and any TAXII 2.1-compatible platform. Our onboarding portal provides copy-ready curl + Python import scripts per SIEM. Average setup time: under 15 minutes.
What CVEs do you cover, and how fast?
All CRITICAL (CVSS 9.0+) CVEs in widely-deployed enterprise software receive God Mode treatment within 24–48h of NVD/CISA publication. HIGH (7.0–8.9) CVEs in enterprise infrastructure within 72h. Enterprise plan subscribers receive a 4-hour SLA on CRITICAL advisories with immediate webhook notification.
