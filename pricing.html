Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get an API key?

Email iambivash.bn@gmail.com with your desired tier. API keys are provisioned within 24 hours for Professional and within 4 hours for Enterprise/MSSP.

What SIEM platforms does SENTINEL APEX integrate with?

Native STIX/TAXII 2.1 integration works with Microsoft Sentinel, Splunk ES, IBM QRadar, Palo Alto XSOAR, and any SIEM supporting TAXII. See API Docs for connector setup guides.

Is there a trial period for paid plans?

Professional plans include a 14-day free trial. Enterprise and MSSP plans include a 30-day proof-of-concept period. Contact us to arrange a POC environment.

What data is covered by the SLA?

The SLA covers API availability, TAXII feed delivery, dashboard uptime, and data freshness. Full details at SLA page.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. Annual subscriptions are subject to a 30-day notice period. No cancellation fees.
