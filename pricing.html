Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 7-day free trial work? +
Sign up at the upgrade page. You get full PRO access for 7 days at no cost. After 7 days, choose to continue at $49/month (monthly) or $39/month (annual, billed $470/yr) — or drop back to Community. No credit card required to start.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), PayPal, NEFT/RTGS, AmazonPay, BNB/USDT crypto, and bank transfer. GSTIN: 21ARKPN8270G1ZP. GST invoices available for all enterprise plans.
How are detection rules generated? +
Our APEX Detection Engineering engine generates Sigma rules, KQL queries and Suricata signatures for every advisory based on vulnerability class, affected product and observed attack patterns. Rules are updated with each pipeline run (every 6–8 hours).
Can my MSSP resell this intelligence? +
Yes. Our MSSP white-label program lets you brand the dashboard and API under your own name and resell to your clients. Contact us via WhatsApp at +91 8179881447 or email enterprise@cyberdudebivash.com for partner pricing.
What SLA do you offer? +
Enterprise and MSSP plans include a 99.9% uptime SLA. The pipeline runs every 6–8 hours with automated health checks. Critical alerts are pushed via Telegram within 15 minutes of detection.
Is there a student or researcher discount? +
Yes. Students and independent security researchers get 50% off PRO ($24.50/month, ~₹2,050). Send proof of enrollment or your security research profile to enterprise@cyberdudebivash.com.
How does annual billing work? +
Select "Annual" above to switch to annual pricing — you save 20% versus month-to-month. PRO drops to $39/mo ($470 billed once per year), Enterprise SOC to $399/mo ($4,788/yr), and MSSP to $1,599/mo ($19,188/yr). Annual plans are pre-paid for 12 months and include price-lock protection — your rate will not increase at renewal. Cancel with 30 days' notice before the renewal date. Invoice and GST receipt provided immediately on purchase.
Can I switch between monthly and annual mid-cycle? +
Yes — you can upgrade from monthly to annual at any time and we will credit the unused portion of your current month. To downgrade from annual to monthly, the switch takes effect at the next renewal date. Email enterprise@cyberdudebivash.com to request billing changes.
How does local currency pricing work? +
Use the currency selector above the pricing cards to view indicative prices in USD, INR, EUR, or GBP. All subscriptions are billed in USD at the prevailing exchange rate unless a local invoicing arrangement is made — email enterprise@cyberdudebivash.com. Indian customers receive GST invoices in INR equivalent (18% GST applicable, GSTIN: 21ARKPN8270G1ZP). EUR and GBP prices are indicative at approx. $1 = €0.92 = £0.79 = ₹83.5 and are updated periodically — actual billing uses the rate at time of payment.
