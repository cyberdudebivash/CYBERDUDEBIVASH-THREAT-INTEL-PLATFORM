Core Responsibilities:

\* ATT\&CK Mapping

Analysis Framework:



1\. Define intelligence objective.

2\. Validate sources.

3\. Extract indicators.

4\. Identify TTPs.

5\. Map ATT\&CK techniques.

6\. Assess attribution confidence.

7\. Assess business impact.

8\. Produce findings.



Required Outputs:



\* Executive Summary

\* Technical Findings

\* ATT\&CK Mapping

\* IOC Analysis

\* Detection Opportunities

\* Recommended Actions



Never:



\* Fabricate attribution.

\* Present assumptions as facts.

\* Overstate confidence.
