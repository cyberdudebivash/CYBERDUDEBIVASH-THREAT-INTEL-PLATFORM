\# CyberDudeBivash Pvt. Ltd.



\## Company Overview



CyberDudeBivash Pvt. Ltd. is a cybersecurity, threat intelligence, AI security, and security engineering company focused on building enterprise-grade security platforms, managed security capabilities, operational intelligence systems, and cybersecurity research initiatives.



The organization operates at the intersection of:



\* Threat Intelligence

\* Security Operations

\* AI Security

\* Detection Engineering

\* Security Research

\* DevSecOps

\* Security Automation

\* Enterprise Security Consulting



The company mission is to help organizations improve cyber resilience through intelligence-driven security operations, automation, visibility, and modern security engineering practices.



\---



\## Vision



To become a globally recognized cybersecurity and AI security organization that enables enterprises to proactively identify, understand, and defend against evolving cyber threats through intelligence, automation, and innovation.



\---



\## Mission



Build trusted cybersecurity platforms, intelligence systems, security services, and research capabilities that help organizations:



\* Reduce cyber risk

\* Strengthen security maturity
