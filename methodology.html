Business Impact Score (BIS) Framework

The BIS is a composite score (0-10) that synthesizes: CVSS base score (40% weight), EPSS exploitation probability (25%), IOC density (10%), FAIR loss estimate relative to sector median (15%), and regulatory compliance exposure (10%). BIS 9.0+ = CRITICAL board-level reporting required.
