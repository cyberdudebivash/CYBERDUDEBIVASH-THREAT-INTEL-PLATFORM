COMMON QUESTIONS

MSSP Partner FAQ

Do clients know they're using SENTINEL APEX?

No. The white-label configuration replaces all SENTINEL APEX branding with your company's branding — logo, name, color, email domain, and portal URL. Your clients see only your brand. The underlying intelligence quality is unchanged.

How do I handle client API keys?

You generate sub-tenant API keys from your MSSP admin panel. Each key is scoped to a single tenant and cannot access another tenant's data. You can revoke, rotate, or restrict keys at any time without contacting us.

Is there a limit on how many clients I can manage?

MSSP PRO supports up to 250 active sub-tenants at no additional per-tenant fee. Need more? Contact your MSSP Success Manager — we can provision higher limits for volume partners.

What's my commercial model with clients?

You control your own commercial terms with clients. SENTINEL APEX is your backend. You can charge clients per-seat, per-month, or bundle it into your existing MSSP retainer. SENTINEL APEX pays you commission on the client's billing — you keep your own margin on top.

What support do I get as an MSSP partner?

Dedicated MSSP Success Manager, priority support (4h SLA), quarterly business reviews, co-marketing opportunities (joint case studies, certified partner badge), and optional 1-hour onboarding call per new Enterprise client you add.

How is commission paid?

Commission is calculated monthly on the net billing of all active clients under your MSSP account. Payment via bank transfer, PayPal, or UPI. Minimum payout threshold: $100. Paid within 15 days of month-end.
