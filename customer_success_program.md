# CYBERDUDEBIVASH® SENTINEL APEX
## Customer Success Program
**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-05-29

---

## PROGRAM MISSION

Ensure every paying customer achieves measurable security outcomes — reduced detection time, improved coverage, faster analyst workflows — and attributes those outcomes to SENTINEL APEX.

**Success = Customer outcomes, not platform utilization.**

---

## SECTION 1 — CUSTOMER SUCCESS MOTION BY TIER

### Free / Pro Tier — Digital-Led Success
- Automated onboarding email sequences (Days 1, 3, 7, 14, 30)
- In-app onboarding checklist with progress tracking
- Self-service documentation and video walkthroughs
- Community support forum access
- Monthly automated usage digest email
- No dedicated CSM — scale via automation

### Enterprise Tier — CSM-Led Success
- Named Customer Success Manager assigned at contract signature
- 60-minute onboarding kickoff call within 24h of activation
- 30/60/90 day success plan with measurable milestones
- Monthly check-in calls
- Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs)
- Dedicated Slack channel (#sentinel-apex-[companyname])
- Priority support routing

### MSSP Tier — Partner Success
- Named Partner Success Manager
- Weekly operational check-ins
- Tenant health dashboard access
- Monthly partner business review
- Quarterly expansion planning
- Dedicated escalation hotline

## SECTION 3 — QUARTERLY BUSINESS REVIEW (QBR) FRAMEWORK

### QBR Agenda (60 minutes)
1. **Platform performance summary** (10 min)
   - Uptime, feed freshness, API availability
2. **Intelligence impact review** (15 min)
   - Advisories delivered, IOCs processed, rules generated
   - CVEs detected before vendor patch available
   - Threat actors tracked relevant to customer sector
3. **SOC outcome metrics** (10 min)
   - Investigations completed
   - Mean time to detect (MTTD) trend
   - Alert triage rate
4. **ATT&CK coverage progress** (10 min)
   - Coverage heatmap: before vs. current quarter
   - Top 5 gap techniques with remediation roadmap
5. **Roadmap preview** (10 min)
   - Upcoming platform capabilities
   - Customer-requested features status
6. **Success plan for next quarter** (5 min)
   - Updated milestones
   - Expansion opportunities discussed

### QBR Deliverable
A branded PDF report covering all above sections, delivered to CISO and Security Director 24h before the call.

## SECTION 4 — CUSTOMER HEALTH SCORING

### Health Score Dimensions (100 points total)

| Dimension | Weight | Healthy Signal |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| API Activity | 20 pts | ≥1 API call/day |
| Dashboard Logins | 15 pts | ≥3 logins/week |
| Intelligence Consumption | 20 pts | ≥10 advisories viewed/week |
| Detection Rule Deployment | 15 pts | ≥5 rules active in SIEM |
| Support Ticket Sentiment | 10 pts | No open P1/P2 tickets |
| Billing Standing | 10 pts | No overdue invoices |
| Engagement (Calls/Reviews) | 10 pts | QBR attended last quarter |

**Score Bands:**
- 80–100: **HEALTHY** — Expansion opportunity
- 60–79: **STABLE** — Maintain standard cadence
- 40–59: **AT RISK** — Proactive outreach required
- 0–39: **CRITICAL** — Executive intervention, churn prevention mode

| Feedback Type | Trigger | Method |
