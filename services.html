FAQ

Common Questions

Do you sign NDAs before engagements? ▼
Yes — NDA is standard for all enterprise and direct engagements. I can work with your NDA template or provide a standard mutual NDA. For Upwork/Fiverr engagements, platform confidentiality terms apply.
What time zone / availability do you offer? ▼
Based in India (IST, UTC+5:30). Available for calls with European and North American clients — I accommodate overlapping hours for kickoff calls, weekly reviews, and critical incident response. IR retainer includes 24/7 async escalation with 4-hour initial triage SLA.
What data do you need access to for threat hunting engagements? ▼
Depending on scope: SIEM query access (read-only), EDR API or log exports, network flow summaries, and DNS/proxy logs. All access is minimal-privilege, time-limited, and documented in the SOW. No raw credential access required — I work with read-only API keys or log file exports where possible.
How do detection rule deliverables integrate with my SIEM? ▼
All rules are delivered in platform-native format: KQL for Microsoft Sentinel, SPL saved searches for Splunk, EQL for Elastic, and raw Sigma (which you can transpile to any platform). Each rule includes field mapping notes for common log schemas (Windows Event Logs, Sysmon, CEF, LEEF) so your team can deploy immediately with minimal tuning.
Can I purchase detection packs without a service engagement? ▼
Yes — all detection packs, AI security kits, and defense kits are available as standalone instant-download purchases via Gumroad or direct purchase. Visit the SENTINEL APEX Store → to purchase without any service engagement required.
Do you offer payment plans for enterprise contracts? ▼
Yes — enterprise contracts can be structured with monthly milestone payments, quarterly billing, or annual prepay with a discount. Contact directly to discuss payment structure that works for your procurement process.
