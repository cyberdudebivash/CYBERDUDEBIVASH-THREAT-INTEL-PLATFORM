COMMON QUESTIONS
Enterprise FAQ
How quickly can we go live with the Enterprise API? ▼
Most enterprise API deployments are live within 24 hours of contract signing. We provide an onboarding call, test credentials, and direct integration support for your SIEM platform. Custom feeds may take 3–5 business days for sector-specific filtering setup.
Do you support on-premise / air-gapped deployments? ▼
Yes, for qualifying enterprise contracts. We offer STIX 2.1 + TAXII 2.1 polling endpoints that work with on-premise SIEM configurations, and can provide periodic encrypted dump files for air-gapped environments. Contact us to discuss your architecture.
Can we get a sector-specific threat feed (e.g. finance-only)? ▼
Yes. Enterprise tier includes custom filtering by sector, threat actor, malware family, CVE severity, or MITRE technique. We work with your SOC team to tune the feed in the first 30 days. Additional customization is available as a managed service add-on.
What SLA do you provide for enterprise customers? ▼
Enterprise contracts include a 99.9% uptime SLA in writing. Support response: P0/Critical issues within 2 hours, general support within 4 business hours. A named account manager is assigned to all annual enterprise accounts.
Is whitelabeling / OEM available? ▼
Yes, for MSSP and security product vendors. Our threat intelligence API can be embedded into your product under your own brand. API response headers, attribution, and endpoint namespacing can all be customized. Minimum commitment is 6 months. Contact enterprise sales for pricing.
Do you offer a free trial before enterprise commitment? ▼
Yes. We offer a 14-day enterprise trial with full API access (STIX export, AI endpoints, unlimited calls) for qualified prospects. Submit the contact form above and mention "trial" in your message. We'll provision trial credentials on the same business day.
