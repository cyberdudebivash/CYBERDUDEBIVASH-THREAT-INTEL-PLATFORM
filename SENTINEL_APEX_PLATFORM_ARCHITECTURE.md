# 1. Executive Overview

Sentinel APEX™ is an automated **threat intelligence production platform** designed to continuously collect, analyze, correlate, and publish cyber threat intelligence.

The platform transforms raw security signals into actionable intelligence through a multi-stage pipeline composed of specialized analysis engines.

Primary goals:

• Detect emerging threats early  
• Identify zero-day exploitation signals  
• correlate global attack telemetry  
• generate defensive playbooks  
• produce machine-readable intelligence  

Sentinel APEX is designed to operate as a **continuous threat intelligence engine**.

# 2. Platform Objectives

Sentinel APEX supports the following operational goals:

### Continuous Intelligence Production
Automated pipelines produce threat intelligence without manual intervention.

### Early Threat Detection
Detection of:

• emerging vulnerabilities  
• exploit development  
• attacker infrastructure  
• attack campaigns  

### Defensive Actionability
Generated intelligence includes:

• IOCs  
• threat analysis  
• detection guidance  
• response playbooks  

### Machine Readable Intelligence
All intelligence is exportable in structured formats:

• STIX 2.1
• JSON intelligence reports
