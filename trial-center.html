Frequently Asked Questions
Is a credit card required to start the trial?+
No credit card is required. Trials are activated after manual payment verification via UPI, PayPal, NEFT, or crypto. There is no automated recurring billing — you decide to renew when your trial ends, with no surprise charges.
How long does trial activation take?+
Payment verification typically completes within 2 business hours. Your API key is generated automatically upon approval and delivered by email immediately — no manual step needed on your end. Support operates IST business hours with best-effort off-hours coverage.
What happens when my trial ends?+
Your API key is suspended at trial expiry. You'll receive 7-day and 3-day reminder emails. To continue, submit a new payment via your preferred method and your key is reactivated within 2 hours. No data is lost during the gap.
Can I upgrade from PRO trial to Enterprise during the trial period?+
Yes. Email enterprise@cyberdudebivash.com and we'll upgrade your trial tier within 2 hours at no additional cost during the evaluation window.
Does the trial include a SIEM integration call?+
Enterprise and MSSP trials include a live onboarding call (2 hr and 3 hr respectively) where we connect your SIEM and push your first detection rules. PRO trials include async documentation, video guides, and Slack community support.
Is the trial really free — what's the catch?+
There is no catch. The trial requires a nominal one-time proof-of-intent payment (amount varies by tier) that is credited toward your first paid month if you subscribe. If you don't subscribe, no recurring charge is made. We use this to prevent API abuse rather than credit card holds.
Can my team share one trial account?+
Yes. A single API key can be shared across your SOC team members. Enterprise trials support up to 5 named users with individual audit logs. MSSP evaluations can spin up 5 sub-tenant environments for testing client deployments.
What payment methods are supported?+
We accept UPI (GPay / PhonePe / Paytm), PayPal, bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS/SWIFT), and select cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, USDT). Indian customers pay + 18% GST. For invoicing requirements, email billing@cyberdudebivash.com.
