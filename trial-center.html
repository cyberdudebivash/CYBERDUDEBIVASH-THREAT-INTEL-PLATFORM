Frequently Asked Questions
Is a credit card required? +
No. Trials are activated after manual payment verification via UPI, PayPal, NEFT, or crypto. There is no automated recurring billing — you choose to renew when your trial ends.
How long does trial activation take? +
Payment verification typically takes under 4 business hours. Your API key is generated automatically upon approval and emailed to you immediately. No manual step required on your end.
What happens when my trial ends? +
Your API key is automatically suspended. You'll receive 7-day and 3-day reminder emails. To continue, submit a new payment via UPI/PayPal/NEFT/Crypto and your key is reactivated within 4 hours.
Can I switch from PRO trial to Enterprise trial? +
Yes. Contact us at enterprise@cyberdudebivash.in and we'll upgrade your trial tier within 2 hours at no additional cost during the evaluation period.
Does the trial include a SIEM integration call? +
Enterprise trials include a 2-hour onboarding call where we connect your SIEM and deploy your first detection rules live. PRO trials include async documentation + video guides.
