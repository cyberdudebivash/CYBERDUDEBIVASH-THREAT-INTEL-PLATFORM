## EXPANSION PHILOSOPHY

The best expansion revenue comes from customers who already trust the platform. Expansion is earned through consistent delivery of value — not through sales pressure. When a customer is getting ROI, the upsell conversation is about removing constraints, not creating new ones.

**Expansion model:** Land → Deliver → Expand → Deepen → Partner

## SECTION 3 — EXPANSION PLAYBOOKS

### Playbook A — Free to Pro Conversion

**Target:** Free accounts with >10 advisory views in 30 days but no API calls.

**Trigger:** 10+ advisory views without Pro feature use.

**Outreach:** "You've been reviewing advisories in SENTINEL APEX — Pro gives you full IOC feeds, detection rules, and SIEM integration for $49/month. That's less than one analyst-hour per week."

**Trial offer:** 14-day Pro trial with full access, no credit card required.

**Conversion target:** 15% of engaged Free users convert to Pro.

### Playbook B — Pro to Enterprise Upgrade

**Target:** Pro accounts with API usage >60% of limit OR team size ≥3.

**Framing:** "You're operating at Pro tier limits — Enterprise gives you 4× the API capacity, full KQL/SPL detection packs, 25 analyst seats, and a dedicated CSM. The ROI on a 5-analyst team paying $499/month is measured in analyst hours saved."

**ROI calculation to share:**
- 5 analysts × 1 hour saved per day × $75/hour × 250 working days = $93,750/year saved
- Enterprise annual cost = $4,999
- ROI = 1,775%

### Playbook C — Enterprise to MSSP Conversion

**Target:** Enterprise accounts with 2+ client environments or internal team serving multiple business units.

**Qualifying questions:**
- "Are you currently managing intelligence for multiple client organizations?"
- "Do you have internal business units that need isolated threat intelligence?"
- "Are you reselling or considering reselling security services?"

**MSSP value proposition:**
- Tenant isolation = client data never crosses
- Per-tenant SLA tracking
- White-label reports with client branding
- Multi-tenant billing visibility
- 10–25x lower cost than competitive MSSP intelligence platforms

### Playbook D — AI Security Module Upsell

**Target:** Enterprise accounts where AI/ML initiatives are identified.

**Trigger conditions:**
- Customer mentions ChatGPT, LLMs, or AI in support ticket
- Customer's company has announced AI investment publicly
- Customer's security team asks about prompt injection
- QBR discussion includes AI risk

**Framing:** "Given your AI initiatives, SENTINEL APEX's AI Security Intelligence module gives your team real-time tracking of prompt injection campaigns, model extraction attacks, and AI system vulnerabilities — before they affect your deployment."

## SECTION 5 — EXPANSION GOVERNANCE

### Expansion Review Cadence
- **Weekly:** CSM identifies top 5 expansion opportunities from health score data
- **Monthly:** Revenue operations reviews expansion pipeline vs. forecast
- **Quarterly:** AE + CSM joint expansion planning per strategic account

### Expansion Rules of Engagement
1. Never propose an upgrade without demonstrating the current tier is being maxed out
2. ROI calculation must accompany every tier upgrade proposal
3. MSSP conversion requires SE qualification call before proposal
4. No expansion outreach within 60 days of a P1 support ticket
5. Customer health score must be >60 before expansion motion begins
