### Recommendation 2: Raise Prices Before More Marketing

**Current PRO: $49/month. Recommended: $99/month.**
**Current Enterprise: $180/$499 (inconsistent). Recommended: $999/month.**

The market does not respect a $49 threat intelligence platform. Enterprise buyers have $500–2,000/month CTI budgets. Pricing at $49 signals "this is not an enterprise product." The FAIR model in every advisory proves APEX prevents $250K–$1.5M breaches. A $999/month subscription that prevents a $1M breach has an ROI of 83× per year. Price accordingly.

**Board action:** Approve price increase to $99 PRO / $999 Enterprise / $1,999 MSSP before any paid customer acquisition begins. Early adopter discount (first 20 customers pay today's $49) can be offered as a conversion incentive.
