Data Quality Commitments

Intelligence Freshness

  • Threat intelligence is refreshed at minimum once every 24 hours on all paid tiers.
  • Enterprise and MSSP tiers receive TAXII push notifications within 30 minutes of new advisory publication.
  • Historical intelligence archive: 30 days (Professional), 365 days (Enterprise/MSSP).

IOC Accuracy

  • All IOCs are validated through the SENTINEL APEX IOC Quality Hardener before publication.
  • False positive rate target: <2% on network indicators (IP, domain, URL).
  • EPSS scores are validated against CVSS severity for statistical consistency (v161.0).
  • CVSS scores are sourced from NVD REST API v2 with automated backfill for new CVEs.

STIX Compliance

  • All intelligence bundles are STIX 2.1 compliant and TAXII 2.1 compatible.
  • MITRE ATT&CK technique mappings are evidence-based (v16 framework).

Exclusions & Force Majeure

The following are excluded from SLA calculations: scheduled maintenance (72h notice), third-party API outages (NVD, FIRST EPSS, CISA), DNS propagation events, DDoS attacks exceeding Cloudflare mitigations, and governmental/legal orders requiring service suspension.

SLA Credit Procedure

To claim SLA credits, submit a request to iambivash.bn@gmail.com within 30 days of the incident with: incident date/time, observed degradation, and your subscription tier. Credits are applied to the next billing cycle and cannot be exchanged for cash.
