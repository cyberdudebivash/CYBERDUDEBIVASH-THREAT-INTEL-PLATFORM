Frequently Asked Questions

What is the SENTINEL APEX Threat Intelligence Hub?
The SENTINEL APEX Threat Intelligence Hub is a real-time global feed of cyber threats operated by CYBERDUDEBIVASH PRIVATE LIMITED. It aggregates intelligence from 74+ sources covering APT campaigns, ransomware operations, malware families, IOC indicators, and threat actor profiles — enriched with AI-powered correlation and MITRE ATT&CK mapping.
How often is threat intelligence updated?
Threat intelligence is updated continuously. CVE and CISA KEV data is refreshed every 15 minutes. IOC feeds are ingested every 6 hours. Campaign and threat actor profiles are updated within 24 hours of new intelligence becoming available.
What data formats does SENTINEL APEX support?
SENTINEL APEX supports STIX 2.1, TAXII 2.1, MISP, JSON, CSV, Sigma rules, and YARA rules for threat intelligence export and integration with SIEM, SOAR, and EDR platforms.
Is there a free tier?
Yes. SENTINEL APEX offers a free tier with access to core threat intelligence features. Enterprise plans with full API access, STIX 2.1 export, and dedicated support start at $49/month. See pricing for details.
Who operates SENTINEL APEX?
SENTINEL APEX is operated by CYBERDUDEBIVASH PRIVATE LIMITED, a cybersecurity company founded by Bivash Kumar Nayak, headquartered in Jajpur Road, Odisha, India. The company specializes in AI-driven threat intelligence and autonomous SOC platforms.
