# CYBERDUDEBIVASH® SENTINEL APEX
## Customer Journey Map
**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-05-29

---

## JOURNEY OVERVIEW

This map covers the complete enterprise customer lifecycle from first awareness through multi-year strategic partnership, documenting every touchpoint, emotion, action, and system interaction.

---

## STAGE 1 — AWARENESS
**Timeline:** Pre-sales | **Customer emotion:** Skeptical / Curious

### Customer Actions
- Reads blog / LinkedIn post about threat intelligence gap in SOC
- Searches "affordable threat intelligence platform" or "STIX 2.1 API CTI"
- Finds SENTINEL APEX via search, G2 comparison, or peer referral
- Reviews pricing page, reads case study or methodology documentation

### Platform Touchpoints
- SEO-optimized blog content and landing pages
- G2 / Gartner Peer Insights listing
- LinkedIn thought leadership posts
- Free tier sign-up (no credit card required)

### Customer Questions
- "Is this platform legitimate?"
- "Can this compete with Recorded Future at a fraction of the cost?"
- "Do they have real ATT&CK coverage?"

### Success Metric
First visit to `intel.cyberdudebivash.com` → account creation.

---

## STAGE 2 — EVALUATION
**Timeline:** Days 1–30 (trial/free tier) | **Customer emotion:** Testing / Validating

### Customer Actions
- Creates free account
- Reviews live advisory feed
- Checks ATT&CK coverage heatmap
- Downloads sample Sigma/YARA rules
- Makes first API call
- Compares against current intelligence sources

### Platform Touchpoints
- Automated Day 1 welcome email with getting started guide
- In-app onboarding checklist
- Free tier dashboard with upgrade CTAs
- API documentation
- Day 7 email: "How is SENTINEL APEX working for you?"
- Day 14 email: "3 things Enterprise customers do in their first 2 weeks"

### Customer Questions
- "Are the advisories actually better than NVD/CISA free feeds?"
- "Will these Sigma rules work in my environment without modification?"
- "What happens when I upgrade — how long does integration take?"

### Friction Points & Resolutions
| Friction | Resolution |
|---------|-----------|
| Free tier limits feel restrictive | Clear upgrade path with 14-day free trial of Pro |
| API documentation unclear | Embedded code examples in 5 languages |
| First Sigma rule doesn't import cleanly | Integration guide for Splunk/Sentinel/QRadar + support chat |

### Success Metric
Customer makes first API call OR deploys first detection rule.

---

## STAGE 3 — CONVERSION
**Timeline:** Days 15–45 | **Customer emotion:** Committed / Optimistic

### Customer Actions
- Upgrades from Free → Pro or directly to Enterprise
- Completes billing setup
- Receives API key for production tier
- Schedules kickoff call (Enterprise)
- Begins SIEM integration

### Platform Touchpoints
- Upgrade flow (in-app, <3 minutes)
- Order confirmation + invoice email
- Welcome to [tier] email with next steps
- CSM assignment email (Enterprise)
- Kickoff call within 24h (Enterprise)
- Production API key delivery

### Customer Emotions at Conversion
- Optimistic: "I got a good deal — 10× cheaper than Recorded Future"
- Slightly anxious: "I hope this integrates smoothly"
- Expectant: "I need to show ROI to my CISO within 30 days"

### Platform Promise at This Stage
**"You will have your first SIEM alert from SENTINEL APEX intelligence within 60 minutes of activation."**

### Success Metric
Customer receives first advisory in SIEM within 60 minutes of upgrade.

---

## STAGE 4 — ONBOARDING
**Timeline:** Days 1–14 post-conversion | **Customer emotion:** Focused / Occasionally Frustrated

### Customer Actions
- Configures API integration with SIEM
- Sets up webhook for real-time alerts
- Downloads and imports first detection rule pack
- Reviews first ATT&CK coverage baseline
- Adds 2nd analyst to platform
- Completes onboarding checklist

### Platform Touchpoints
- Onboarding checklist in dashboard (progress tracked)
- Integration guide library (Splunk, Sentinel, QRadar, Chronicle)
- Day 2 check-in email: "Quick onboarding win to share?"
- Day 7: First health check (CSM reviews adoption metrics)
- Day 14: CSM sends "2-week summary" — what's been ingested, what's next

### Friction Points & Resolutions
| Friction | Resolution |
|---------|-----------|
| SIEM integration takes longer than expected | SE office hours 3× per week |
| Detection rules need SIEM-specific formatting | Format guides + community forum |
| Customer can't identify relevant advisories | Sector filter configuration walkthrough |
| Webhook delivery failures | Troubleshooting guide + support ticket auto-opened |

### Success Metric
All onboarding checklist items complete by Day 14. Customer confirms first intelligence-derived alert in SIEM.

---

## STAGE 5 — ACTIVATION
**Timeline:** Days 15–90 | **Customer emotion:** Building confidence / Seeing early value

### Customer Actions
- Uses IOC pivot in graph intelligence for first time
- Completes first investigation in SOC workspace
- Shares platform with additional analysts
- Reviews weekly executive brief
- Opens first detection engineering session using APEX rules

### Platform Touchpoints
- Weekly intelligence digest (automated)
- Monthly usage report (automated)
- CSM monthly check-in call
- Feature walkthroughs triggered by first-use milestones
- "First detection" celebration email when SIEM alert fires

### Customer Emotions at This Stage
- "This actually works — I found something I would have missed."
- "My detection engineer is excited about the Sigma rule quality."
- "I need to show this to the CISO."

### Success Metric
Customer completes first full investigation cycle (alert → investigation → close) using SENTINEL APEX as primary intelligence source.

---

## STAGE 6 — ADOPTION
**Timeline:** Months 3–6 | **Customer emotion:** Integrated / Dependent

### Customer Actions
- APEX becomes primary CTI source (replaces or supplements prior sources)
- Weekly ATT&CK coverage reviews normalized
- Monthly executive briefing delivered to CISO
- Threat hunt first conducted using APEX data
- Playbooks referencing APEX IOCs deployed in SOAR

### Platform Touchpoints
- QBR at Month 3 (enterprise)
- ATT&CK coverage improvement report (shows progress since Day 1)
- Analyst milestone badges awarded
- Platform health dashboard in customer environment (API-fed widget)

### Success Metric
Customer's threat intel lead confirms APEX as primary CTI source. APEX referenced in at least one internal security incident report.

---

## STAGE 7 — EXPANSION
**Timeline:** Months 6–12 | **Customer emotion:** Confident / Value-seeking

### Customer Actions
- Evaluates upgrading to next tier
- Adds additional analysts or subsidiaries
- Requests AI Security Intelligence module
- Considers MSSP usage for client environments
- Requests custom threat briefing

### Platform Touchpoints
- Upgrade prompts triggered by usage data
- Expansion call from CSM/AE
- Demo of next-tier capabilities
- ROI report showing year-to-date value delivered
- Add-on catalog in dashboard

### Success Metric
Customer expands ARR by ≥20% at Month 9.

---

## STAGE 8 — RENEWAL
**Timeline:** Month 10–12 | **Customer emotion:** Evaluating / Expecting value justification

### Customer Actions
- Reviews year-end ROI
- Assesses alternatives (low risk if adoption high)
- Discusses renewal terms with CISO/CFO
- Signs renewal + potential expansion

### Platform Touchpoints
- T-90: Renewal alert + CSM outreach
- T-60: Annual ROI report delivered
- T-45: QBR with renewal discussion
- T-30: Renewal proposal (multi-year discount offered)
- T-7: Final confirmation + invoice

### Customer Questions
- "Did we get our money's worth?"
- "What's improving in Year 2?"
- "Can we negotiate annual pricing?"

### Success Metric
Renewal signed before T-30. NRR >110% (expansion included).

---

## STAGE 9 — ADVOCACY
**Timeline:** Month 12+ | **Customer emotion:** Proud / Advocate

### Customer Actions
- Writes G2 review
- Participates in case study
- Refers peer CISOs
- Joins APEX community / advisory board
- Speaks at webinar

### Platform Touchpoints
- Case study invitation
- LinkedIn badge for APEX Champions
- Advisory board invitations for strategic partners
- Referral program ($500 credit per qualified referral)

### Success Metric
Customer acts as reference for ≥1 new prospect.
