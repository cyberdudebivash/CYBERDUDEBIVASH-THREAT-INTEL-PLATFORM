🧠 AI Transparency Statement

What our AI does SENTINEL APEX runs three statistical AI engines (Alpha, Beta, Gamma) that process threat intelligence feeds to detect anomalies, correlate campaigns, and forecast sector risk. All outputs are derived from the SENTINEL APEX feed — no black-box neural networks operate without human-auditable logic.
What our AI does NOT do Our AI does not make autonomous blocking or firewall decisions. It does not access your internal systems. All recommendations are advisory and require human SOC review before action.
Explainability guarantee Every anomaly, campaign, prediction, and escalation output includes: WHY it was flagged, HOW it was scored, WHAT evidence was used, WHICH telemetry channels contributed, and the full confidence derivation chain. No black-box AI outputs are presented to enterprise users.

📊 Threat Scoring Methodology

ENGINE ALPHA — ANOMALY SCORE FORMULA
1
Severity Base: CRITICAL=9.2, HIGH=7.3, MEDIUM=5.1, LOW=2.8
2
EPSS Contribution: raw_epss × 0.8 (EPSS v3 exploit probability)
3
KEV Bonus: +1.5 if CISA KEV confirmed (active exploitation verified)
4
Confidence Adjustment: (confidence_score / 100) × 0.5
5
Composite Score: sum of above, capped at 10.0
GLOBAL RISK INDEX FORMULA
GRI = min(avg_risk × 8 + kev_count × 1.5 + critical_count × 0.5, 100)
