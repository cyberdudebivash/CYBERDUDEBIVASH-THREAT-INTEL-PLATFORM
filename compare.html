Switching from another platform? Common questions
"We already use Recorded Future — why switch?"
SENTINEL APEX is not a full replacement for Recorded Future's analyst-grade strategic intelligence. However, for the majority of SIEM-integrated tactical CTI use cases (IOC feeds, detection rules, STIX bundles), SENTINEL APEX delivers equivalent or better coverage at 5–10% of the cost. Many teams run both — Recorded Future for strategic, APEX for operational.
"CrowdStrike already bundles intelligence with our endpoint."
Falcon Intelligence is tightly coupled to the CrowdStrike endpoint agent and ecosystem. SENTINEL APEX is vendor-neutral, integrates with any SIEM (Sentinel, Splunk, QRadar, Elastic), and generates Sigma rules that work regardless of your EDR. It complements — rather than competes with — your endpoint investment.
"We self-host MISP — it's free."
MISP is powerful but requires DevOps infrastructure, ongoing maintenance, feed curation, and TLP enforcement that absorbs 0.5–1 FTE of engineering effort. SENTINEL APEX delivers managed, curated feeds via API within 2 hours of signup — no servers, no feed maintenance. Many organizations run MISP and SENTINEL APEX together using TAXII 2.1 push.
"We need SOC 2 Type II certification."
SENTINEL APEX is currently undergoing SOC 2 Type II audit (Q1–Q3 2026). In the interim, we provide a SOC 2 bridge letter, security controls documentation, and NIST CSF 2.0 mapping. Enterprise customers can request our security pack via the briefing page.
"Can I import my existing STIX bundles from another platform?"
Yes. SENTINEL APEX's TAXII 2.1 collection endpoint accepts inbound STIX 2.1 objects. You can migrate IOC history, actor profiles, and campaign objects from any STIX 2.1-compliant platform including Recorded Future exports, MISP, and OpenCTI. Contact enterprise@cyberdudebivash.com for a migration walkthrough.
"We're in India — can you issue a GST invoice?"
Yes. SENTINEL APEX is registered under Indian GST (GSTIN: 21ARKPN8270G1ZP). We issue GST-compliant tax invoices in INR, accept UPI (GPay/PhonePe/Paytm), NEFT/RTGS, and PayPal. Indian customers pay listed INR prices + 18% GST. See pricing page for ₹ amounts.
