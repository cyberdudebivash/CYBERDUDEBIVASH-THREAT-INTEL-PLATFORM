Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-08Effective: 2026-05-08GDPR Compliant

Summary: We collect minimal personal data (email, billing info for paid subscribers). We do not sell your data. We do not store intelligence queries beyond access logs. You have the right to access, correct, and delete your data.

1. Data Controller

CyberDudeBivash Pvt. Ltd. ("Company", "we", "us") is the data controller for personal data collected through the CYBERDUDEBIVASH SENTINEL APEX platform (intel.cyberdudebivash.com). Contact: privacy@cyberdudebivash.com

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

  • Account data: Name, email address, organization name (for paid subscribers)
  • Billing data: Payment method details (processed by Stripe — we do not store card numbers), billing address, invoice history
  • Communication data: Support requests, emails sent to intelligence@cyberdudebivash.com
  • API registration data: API key labels, integration names, webhook URLs (for Team/Enterprise tiers)

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Server access logs: IP address, user agent, pages visited, timestamps — retained for 90 days
  • Cloudflare analytics: Aggregate page view counts, CDN cache statistics — no individual tracking, no cookies
  • API usage logs: Endpoint called, response code, timestamp, API key hash (not key itself) — retained for 90 days

2.3 Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels
  • We do not fingerprint browsers or devices
  • We do not collect or store the content of security queries, except as necessary for cached API responses
  • Public visitors to intel.cyberdudebivash.com are not individually tracked beyond server log IP addresses

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, our legal bases for processing personal data are:

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to deliver subscription services and process payments
  • Legitimate interests: Server access logs for security monitoring and abuse prevention; analytics for platform improvement
  • Legal obligation: Retaining transaction records for 7 years as required by Indian tax law
  • Consent: Marketing emails (you can unsubscribe at any time)

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To provision and maintain your subscription account
  • To process payments and issue invoices
  • To send CRITICAL CVE alert emails (subscriber feature)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To detect and prevent abuse, unauthorized access, and API misuse
  • To comply with legal obligations (tax records, court orders)
  • To improve platform performance and content quality (aggregated analytics only)

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. We share data with third parties only as necessary:

  • Stripe: Payment processing. Your billing data is transmitted directly to Stripe under their privacy policy. We receive only transaction confirmation and masked card details.
  • GitHub: Platform content is hosted via GitHub Pages. GitHub processes access logs. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • Cloudflare: CDN and security services. Cloudflare processes IP addresses and request metadata. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose data in response to valid legal process (court orders, law enforcement requests) after reasonable verification.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active, then 12 months after account deletion (to resolve disputes and for legal compliance)
  • Transaction/billing records: 7 years (Indian tax requirement)
  • Server access logs: 90 days, then automatically purged
  • API usage logs: 90 days, then automatically purged
  • Support communications: 2 years, then deleted

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request your account data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing pending dispute resolution
  • Withdrawal of consent: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@cyberdudebivash.com. We will respond within 30 days. Identity verification may be required.

8. International Data Transfers

The Platform is operated from India. Subscriber data may be processed on servers located in India or the United States (via GitHub and Cloudflare). For EU/EEA subscribers, transfers to the US are covered by Cloudflare's and GitHub's EU Standard Contractual Clauses. We do not directly transfer EU personal data outside of these covered arrangements.

9. Cookies

The Platform does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. The only storage we use is sessionStorage in your browser for caching status page data locally for 5 minutes — this data never leaves your browser and is cleared when you close the tab.

10. Children's Privacy

The Platform is intended for enterprise and professional security use. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

11. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including: HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit; access controls limiting personal data access to authorized personnel; regular review of third-party sub-processors' security posture; GitHub's built-in secret scanning to prevent inadvertent credential exposure.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. Material changes will be communicated to subscribers via email at least 14 days before taking effect. The current version is always available at intel.cyberdudebivash.com/privacy.html.

13. Contact and Complaints

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights: privacy@cyberdudebivash.com

If you are in the EU/EEA and believe we have not handled your data appropriately, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.
